chennai super kings



Chennai Super Kings (CSK) is a professional Twenty20 cricket franchise based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, playing in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2008, the team is known for its passionate fan base and has a history of success, including five IPL titles (2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, 2023). CSK, captained by Ruturaj Gaikwad and coached by Stephen Fleming, has also won the Champions League Twenty20 twice. They are considered one of the most successful IPL franchises, known for their strong team spirit and strategic gameplay.

RUTURAJ

Ruturaj Gaikwad is an Indian international cricketer who captains the Maharashtra cricket team in T20 and List A formats, and is the current captain of Chennai Super Kings in the Indian Premier League (IPL). He made his international debut for India in July 2021 against Sri Lanka and captained the Indian team to a gold medal at the 2022 Asian Games.

JADEGA

Ravindra Jadeja is an all-rounder who has played for the Chennai Super Kings (CSK) in the Indian Premier League (IPL). He is known for his left-arm orthodox bowling and his contributions with the bat, particularly in the slog overs. Jadeja joined CSK in 2012 and has been a key player for the team, contributing to their success.
